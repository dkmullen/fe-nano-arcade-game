Click any image to choose your player anytime.

HOW TO PLAY: Use the arrow keys to move your player up across the grass and the stones to the water without getting hit by a bug. You get THREE LIVES to start with, but each time you reach the water, you earn an extra life, AND 200 POINTS. You get 100 POINTS for each treasure you pick up along the way.

This game keeps track of your score for the current game and also your high score. NOTE that the high score RESETS if you close or refresh the browser tab.
